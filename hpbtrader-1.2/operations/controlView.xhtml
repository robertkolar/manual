Control View

Control view is the main window of HPB Trader web front end. Through the control view an operator monitors the performance of trading strategies and configures series, strategies and parameters.

Control view is split into four distinct areas:
  • Toolbar: contains various buttons for controlling IB connection, series, strategies and order management
  • Series table: a list of active or inactive series, currently active strategies and status/performance overview
  • Real-time quotes: real-time quotes for selected series, getting instantly updated through IB data feed
  • Recent orders: orders placed in a simulated mode or sent to IB Gateway for all series and strategies

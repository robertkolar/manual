Quotes

Historical quotes get automatically and periodically collected for all defined series and intervals. In case of any loss of connectivity to the quote feed they get automatically backfilled in the next periodic update. A backfill can also be performed on demand by selecting the series and clicking the backfill button in the toolbar of the control view.

Quotes table consists of the following columns:
  • Date: the end date/time of the quote bar
  • Open: the opening price of the quote bar interval
  • High: the highest price reached during the quote bar interval
  • Low: the lowest price reached during the quote bar interval
  • Close: the closing price of the quote bar interval
  • Volume: the volume traded during the quote bar interval

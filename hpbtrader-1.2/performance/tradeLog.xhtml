Trade Log

Trade logs table displays a series of snapshots of the trade state during the lifetime of the trade. Trade logs table is dynamically updated with the trade data of the trade selected in the trades table. A new log entry is added when any trade event takes place. A graphical representation of the trade log is also displayed.

Trade logs table columns:
  • Log Date: date and time of the log entry
  • Pos: trade position at the time of the log entry
  • Stop: strategy stop price at the time of the log entry
  • Price: trading instrument price at the time of the log entry
  • Target: strategy target at the time of the log entry
  • Unrl PL: trade unrealized profit or loss at the time of the log entry
  • Rlzd PL: trade realized profit or loss at the time of the log entry
  • Status: trade status at the time of the log entry, one of: initOpen, open, initClose, closed, cncClosed, errClosed

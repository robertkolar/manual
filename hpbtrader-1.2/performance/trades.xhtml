Trades

Trade tab of the performance view shows the list of all trades that took place during the live or simulated strategy operation. A trade can either be long or short and is related to at least two orders - the opening and the closing order. Trade PL chart is a graphical represantation of a cummulative PL for every trade during the lifetime of the strategy.

Trades table columns:
  • Strategy: ticker symbol, interval, trading strategy name
  • Typ: S - short or L - long
  • Quant: quantity traded
  • TPos: trade position, -quant or +quant
  • Date InitOpen: date and time of the trade initialization
  • Date Closed: date and time of the trade close
  • Open: the price of the trading instrument at the trade open
  • Close: the price of the trading instrument at the trade close
  • InitStop: initial stop set by the strategy
  • Stop: the final stop at the trade close time set by the strategy
  • Target: price target set by the strategy
  • Rlzd PL: realized profit or loss
  • Status: trade status, one of: initOpen, open, initClose, closed, cncClosed, errClosed
